# CSE 325 Final Project Brainstorming

## 1. GameVault -- Video Game Backlog Manager

A personal game library where users can organize the games they own or
want to play. Users can register, log in, add games, edit or delete
entries, rate games, track their playing status (Wishlist, Playing,
Completed, etc.), and search or filter their collection.

**Target audience:** Casual and enthusiast gamers who want to manage
their personal backlog.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. PantryPal -- Home Pantry & Grocery Manager

A household management app that helps users keep track of pantry items
and grocery shopping. Users can manage food inventory, record expiration
dates, create shopping lists, and categorize products.

**Target audience:** Individuals, couples, and families who want to
reduce food waste and stay organized when shopping.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. StudyHub -- Assignment & Study Planner

A productivity app that helps students organize coursework and
deadlines. Users can manage courses, assignments, exams, and study
sessions, mark tasks as completed, and filter items by class or due
date.

**Target audience:** College and university students looking for a
simple academic planner.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. FitTrack -- Workout & Fitness Log

A personal fitness tracker that allows users to record workouts and
monitor their exercise routine. Users can create workout entries,
organize exercises by category, log sets and repetitions, and review
their workout history.

**Target audience:** People who exercise regularly and want an easy way
to track their training progress without the complexity of a full
fitness platform.
